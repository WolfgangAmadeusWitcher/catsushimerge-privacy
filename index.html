Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 29, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Cat Sushi Merge ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

Information Collected Automatically

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Through this service, certain information may be collected automatically, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • App usage data and interactions with advertisements
  • Diagnostic information

Information We Do Not Collect

Our App does not:

  • Require user registration or accounts
  • Collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number
  • Access your contacts, camera, microphone, or location
  • Store any personal data on our servers

How We Use Information

The information collected through advertising services is used to:

  • Display relevant advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve ad targeting and user experience

Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. For more information, please visit:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob Policies

Children's Privacy

Cat Sushi Merge is designed to be suitable for all ages and complies with the Google Play Families Policy. We configure our advertising services to:

  • Show only child-appropriate ad content (G-rated)
  • Enable child-directed treatment (COPPA compliance)
  • Request only non-personalized advertisements
  • Comply with GDPR requirements for minors

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The advertising shown in our App is configured to be appropriate for all age groups.

Data Storage

Game progress (such as high scores and unlocked items) is stored locally on your device using standard device storage. This data is not transmitted to any external servers and remains entirely on your device.

Your Choices

You can limit ad tracking on your device:

  • Android: Go to Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

You can also reset your advertising identifier or delete the App at any time to remove locally stored data.

Data Security

All data transmitted through advertising services is encrypted using Transport Layer Security (TLS) protocol.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
